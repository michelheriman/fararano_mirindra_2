About SmartSaha

Smallholder Farmers

Cornerstone of Africa's economy. Smallholder farmers are essential to Africa, producing up to 80% of its food, creating jobs for over 60% of the population, and protecting the environment.

The Problem

Smallholder farmers face unpredictable weather, lack of market access, and unfair prices. These issues threaten their livelihood and food security.

Solution Overview

We are building a platform connecting smallholder farmers with private sector actors. Using AI and precision tools, we help boost resilience and productivity.
