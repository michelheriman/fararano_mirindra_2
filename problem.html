Small holder farmers: Conerstone of Africa's economy

Smallholder farmers are essential to Africa, producing up to 80% of its food, creating jobs for over 60% of the population, and contributing significantly to economic growth while protecting the environment.

Problem statment.

Smallholder farmers confront a myriad of challenges, including the unpredictable impacts of climate change, limited access to markets, and the struggle to secure fair prices for their produce. These hurdles threaten their livelihoods and the sustainability of agricultural communities worldwide.

Solution Overview

We are creating a digital platform that connects smallholder farmers directly with the private sector. By incorporating precision agriculture technologies like remote sensing and machine learning, the platform enhances farmers' resilience and productivity.
